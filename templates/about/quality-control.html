For apparel companies, the most critical and important aspect of manufacturing operations is Quality control and management. An apparel manufacturer can lose 15 to 30 percent of their total CMT costs because their garments are not done right in the first place.

The JUSTGETIT Quality Management Software (QMS) offers an extensive suite of tools and capabilities necessary to cost-effectively manage quality issues while streamlining manufacturing processes. JUSTGETIT AI-based predictive quality control system notifies quality control teams of emerging production defects that are likely to cause product quality issues. It also predicts the possible defects which may come from production lines or even from operators. Defects can include deviations from pacifications, subtle abnormalities in measurement, change in construction, and more. Quality control checks can be put in place before actual production by attending to these issues predicted early by JUSTGETIT AI. It will help to drive tighter cost control by allowing manufacturers to identify and eliminate poor quality and high production costs in areas such as rejects, unscheduled machine downtime, and returns or reworking.
