Who are we?

We are a diverse team of individuals united by a shared love for technology and sustainability. Our leadership comes with 15+ years of experience in the fashion and IT industries. Our Co-Founders - Pawan Gupta, Abhishek Sharma and Jamil Ahmad - launched Fashinza to solve the existing challenges which make the fashion supply chain inefficient, rigid and unethical.

The age-old industry needs to become fast yet sustainable, data-driven, and transparent. It needs to brace itself for a future where trends are ever-changing, threats are ever-evolving and sustainability is a priority. We’re here to act as the bridge that connects the present industry to that future.

Helping build a sustainable ecosystem

We’re key players in an industry that is infamous for posing a threat to the planet. We cannot help but share accountability. Someone has to offer an antidote for the prevalent culture of greenwashing and we consider it to be our responsibility. As a part of our initiative, we encourage our clients to use natural and organic materials and offer eco-friendly packaging options for their products. We furnish sustainability certificates for every stage of production to guarantee sustainable practices across the supply chain. We also actively promote awareness among brands and consumers through our blogs. Our planet and our people are equally important to us, which is why we only partner with manufacturers that offer safe and ethical working conditions to the workers.
