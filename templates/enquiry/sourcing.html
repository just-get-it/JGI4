WHY JUSTGETIT ?

Global Presence

Headquartered in Bangluru (Karnataka), our regional offices are located in Bengaluru and Tirupur with deployed Quality Assurance presence across India. International sourcing is powered through China, Korea and Hong Kong.

One-Stop Sourcing Solution

Timeliness, honesty, transparent communication and early challenge resolution are the founding principles of our service. We offer diverse range of clothing, accessories, footwear, home and leather products.

Young & Dynamic Workforce

With a committed workforce of designers, technical & quality staff, merchandising staff, Auditors and logistics team, we provide concept to delivery solutions. Our Exclusive Ethical Compliance team provides First & Follow up audits in line with Customer & third party audit capability.
